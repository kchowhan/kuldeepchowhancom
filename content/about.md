*Technology Executive · Published Author · Forbes Technology Council Member · Founder*

Kuldeep Chowhan is a technology executive and startup founder with 20+ years of experience building and scaling enterprise platforms at some of the world's most recognized technology companies including **Walmart Global Tech**, **Expedia Group**, **SoFi**, and **Microsoft**. He is the author of *Hands-On Serverless Computing*, published by Packt Publishing, and an official member of the **Forbes Technology Council**.

His expertise spans cloud architecture (AWS, Azure, Google Cloud), serverless computing, DevOps, CI/CD pipeline design, and AI-driven enterprise systems. He is currently building a stealth startup at the intersection of AI agents and enterprise technology.

## Career: From Engineer to VP-Level Technology Leader

Chowhan's career traces a clear trajectory from hands-on engineering to executive leadership across four major technology organizations.

- **Walmart Global Tech - Vice President, Marketplace & Walmart Fulfillment Services Technology.** Led the technology organization powering Walmart's marketplace and fulfillment services platform, one of the largest digital retail operations in the world.
- **SoFi - Principal Software Architect.** Designed scalable system architecture for one of the fastest-growing fintech companies in the United States, focused on banking and financial services infrastructure.
- **Expedia Group - Senior Director of Engineering.** Rose through multiple roles over 5+ years - from Principal Software Developer to Director to Senior Director. Built AWS-based platform tooling consumed by internal teams at scale, created a Platform-as-a-Service (PaaS) tool for automated source code generation and CI/CD pipelines, and designed fully automated deployment systems for Docker containers and AWS Lambda functions.
- **Microsoft - Software Engineer.** Contributed to database and file management software development early in his career.
- **Tata Consultancy Services - IT Analyst.** Began his professional career focusing on IT and business solutions.

## Published Author: Hands-On Serverless Computing

Chowhan authored *Hands-On Serverless Computing: Build, run and orchestrate serverless applications using AWS Lambda, Microsoft Azure Functions, and Google Cloud Functions*, published by **Packt Publishing** in July 2018. The book spans 350 pages across 15 chapters and is available on Amazon, Barnes & Noble, O'Reilly, Apple Books, and Google Books.

The book covers the full serverless landscape across all three major cloud providers. Key topics include:

- Building event-driven applications with AWS Lambda, Azure Functions, and Google Cloud Functions
- Working with API Gateway, DynamoDB, AWS Step Functions, Azure Cosmos DB, and Google Cloud Storage
- Hands-on labs using Node.js, Visual Studio Code, and Postman
- Reference architectures for real-time file processing and serverless web applications

The companion code repository is open-source on GitHub under an MIT license. O'Reilly's platform describes Chowhan as a seasoned cloud architect whose deep knowledge across AWS, Azure, and Google Cloud enables him to guide developers through the intricacies of serverless computing with a clear, hands-on approach.
